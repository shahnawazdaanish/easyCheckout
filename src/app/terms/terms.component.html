Easy Checkout: Terms & Conditions

Please read carefully (Last Updated: April 2019)

1. Account Verification

Click "Remember me" to securely store your credit card info for faster checkout on this and other sites that use Easy Checkout.

2. One-Click Payments

On this computer, when you come back to EASY CHECKOUT or to any other participating site, your credit card info will already be filled in: just click Pay to confirm your purchase.

You can edit the card number if you need to. Easy Checkout will store the one you used most recently.

3. Authentication

Your credit card is linked to your email address and your mobile phone number.

To pay with Easy Checkout on a different computer, or after you log out, just enter your email address during checkout and Easy Checkout will instantly send you a text message to verify your identity.

Message and data rates may apply. Frequency of messages depends on user. For help, reply back with HELP. To stop, reply back with STOP.
